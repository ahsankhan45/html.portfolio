Angela's Recipe for the Best
Cinnamon Rolls

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • 3/4 cup warm milk
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons yeasts
  • 1/4 granulated sugar
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 3 cups bread flour

For the filling:

  • 2/3 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup butter

Instructions

  1. Mix the milk with the yeast,sugar,eggs.
  2. Melt the butter and add to the mixture.
  3. Add in the floor and mix until combined into the dough.
  4. knead the dough for 10 minutes.
  5. Transfer the dough into the large bowl and cover with plastic wrap. Leave
    it somewhere to rise for 2hours.
  6. After the dough has doubled in size, roll it out into the large rectangle.
  7. Melt the butter for the filling and mix in the sugar and cinnamon.
  8. Spred the filling onto the dough then roll the dough into a swiss roll.
  9. cut the roll into 3cm sections and place flat into a baking tray.
  10. preheat the oven to 350F or 180C,then bake the rolls for 20-25min until
    lightly brown.
